About Us

Founded in 2018, PCV & COMPANY. (PCV) is an integrated full service Chartered Accountants firm providing Assurance, Taxation and Advisory services for corporate and co-operative clients at domestic and international level. We are providing broad range of legal, taxation and advisory services to the commercial, industrial and financial communities. We provide all financial and legal services under one roof. our philosophy is of partnering with our clients and not being a distant service provider.

Our services

With the support of independent referents, you can solve many challenges

Audit and Assurance

Audit and assurance services at PCV and Company ensure financial accuracy, compliance, and risk management. Through systematic evaluation, we enhance transparency, detect fraud, and improve internal controls. Reliable audits strengthen business credibility, fostering trust among stakeholders, investors, and regulatory authorities.

Bank Audit

Bank audits ensure compliance, financial accuracy, and risk management in banking operations. We conduct thorough audits to assess internal controls, detect fraud, and ensure regulatory compliance. Our services help maintain transparency, strengthen trust, and enhance the financial integrity of banking institutions.

Taxation

Taxation is a crucial aspect of financial management, ensuring compliance with legal regulations. We provide expert tax planning, filing, and advisory services to optimize tax liabilities, prevent penalties, and maximize savings while ensuring full compliance with evolving tax laws and regulations.

Consultancy

Consultancy services provide expert guidance on finance, taxation, and compliance. We help businesses with strategic planning, risk management, and regulatory adherence. Our insights drive growth, improve efficiency, and ensure informed decision-making for sustainable success in a dynamic business environment.

Corporate Laws

Corporate laws govern business operations, ensuring compliance and ethical practices. We provide expert guidance on company formation, contracts, and regulations. Our services help businesses navigate legal frameworks, mitigate risks, and operate smoothly within corporate governance and statutory requirements.

Company Formation

Company formation involves structuring, registration, and compliance. PCV and Company assists with incorporation, approvals, and documentation, ensuring a smooth setup. Our expertise helps entrepreneurs navigate legal complexities and start operations with
confidence.
